Government of Singapore Initiatives
Smart Nation Initiative

The Smart Nation initiative aims to harness technology to improve the lives of citizens, create more opportunities, and build stronger communities. This initiative focuses on areas such as digital government services, smart urban infrastructure, and enhancing cybersecurity.

Focus Areas
  • Digital Government Services
  • Smart Urban Infrastructure
  • Cybersecurity Enhancements
Key Partners
  • GovTech Singapore
  • National Research Foundation (NRF)
  • Economic Development Board (EDB)
Green Plan 2030

The Singapore Green Plan 2030 is a whole-of-nation movement to advance Singapore’s national agenda on sustainable development. It charts ambitious and concrete targets over the next 10 years, strengthening Singapore’s commitments under the UN's 2030 Sustainable Development Agenda and Paris Agreement.

Focus Areas
  • Carbon Emissions Reduction
  • Green Energy Transition
  • Waste Minimization
Key Partners
  • Ministry of Sustainability and the Environment (MSE)
  • Energy Market Authority (EMA)
  • National Environment Agency (NEA)
SkillsFuture

SkillsFuture is a national movement to provide Singaporeans with the opportunities to develop their fullest potential throughout life, regardless of their starting point. The initiative empowers individuals to take ownership of their skills development and lifelong learning..

Focus Areas
  • Carbon Emissions Reduction
  • Green Energy Transition
  • Waste Minimization
Key Partners
  • Ministry of Sustainability and the Environment (MSE)
  • Energy Market Authority (EMA)
  • National Environment Agency (NEA)

Sustainability and Green Economy

The Government of Singapore is committed to driving sustainable development and transitioning to a green economy. This policy encompasses a range of initiatives aimed at reducing carbon emissions, promoting renewable energy, and fostering green innovation across all sectors of the economy.

  • Cybersecurity Enhancements
